Lab 10: Final Project

Build a complete IoT system

Project Overview

The final project is your opportunity to demonstrate everything you've learned throughout the course by building a complete IoT system that addresses a real-world problem.

Project Requirements

  • Use at least 3 different sensors
  • Include at least 2 actuators
  • Implement wireless communication
  • Include data logging and visualization
  • Address a real agricultural or environmental problem
  • Present your solution to the class
