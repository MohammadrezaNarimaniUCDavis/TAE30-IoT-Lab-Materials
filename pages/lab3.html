Lab 3: Actuators

Control LEDs, motors, and other output devices

Required Materials

  • Arduino Uno or similar microcontroller
  • Breadboard and jumper wires
  • LEDs (various colors)
  • Servo motor
  • Buzzer
  • 220Î© resistors
  • USB cable

Lab Objectives

By the end of this lab, you will be able to:

  • Control multiple LEDs with different patterns
  • Control servo motor position
  • Generate sounds with a buzzer
  • Create interactive feedback systems
  • Combine sensors and actuators
